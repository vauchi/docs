# Recovery Workflow

Step-by-step guide for recovering your contact relationships after losing your device.

## Overview

Vauchi uses **social recovery** - your trusted contacts vouch for your new identity. This is more secure than email/SMS recovery because it requires in-person verification.

## When You Need Recovery

- Lost your phone
- Phone was stolen
- Phone was damaged beyond repair
- Factory reset without backup

## Recovery Process Overview

```
1. Create new identity on new device
2. Generate recovery claim (linking old identity)
3. Meet contacts in person, they vouch for you
4. Collect 3+ vouchers
5. Share recovery proof
6. Contacts update your identity
```

## Step 1: Create New Identity

1. Install Vauchi on your new device
2. Create a new identity (new name, etc.)
3. You now have a new public key

**Important**: Your old identity/keys are gone. Recovery links your new identity to your old one.

## Step 2: Generate Recovery Claim

1. Go to **Recovery** tab
2. Select **Recover** section
3. Tap **Start Recovery Process**
4. Enter your old public key (from backup or memory)
   - Check old backup files
   - Ask contacts - they have your old public key
5. Tap **Create Claim**
6. Copy the claim data

The claim links:
- Your OLD public key (lost device)
- Your NEW public key (current device)

## Step 3: Collect Vouchers

You need vouchers from **3 or more** contacts who knew your old identity.

### Meeting a Contact

1. Meet the contact **in person** (required!)
2. Share your recovery claim:
   - Show claim QR code
   - Or share claim text via messenger
3. They verify it's really you
4. They create a voucher in their app

### For the Contact (Vouching)

1. Go to **Recovery** tab
2. Select **Help Others** section
3. Tap **Vouch for Someone**
4. Paste or scan the recovery claim
5. Verify the claim details
6. **Confirm in-person verification**
7. Tap **Create Voucher**
8. Share the voucher with the recovering person

### Adding Received Vouchers

1. Go to **Recovery** tab
2. Tap **Add Received Voucher**
3. Paste the voucher data
4. See progress update (e.g., "2/3 vouchers")

## Step 4: Recovery Complete

When you have enough vouchers (default: 3):

1. Recovery is marked complete
2. Tap **Copy Recovery Proof**
3. The proof contains all vouchers

## Step 5: Share Recovery Proof

Your recovery proof is automatically uploaded to the relay. Contacts will discover it during sync and can accept your new identity.

For contacts who need manual update:
1. Share your recovery proof with them
2. They verify the proof in their app
3. They update your contact record

## For Contacts: Accepting Recovery

When a contact's recovery proof is discovered:

1. You'll see a notification
2. Open the recovery details
3. Review the vouchers:
   - Mutual contacts are highlighted
   - Unknown vouchers show a warning
4. Accept or reject the recovery

### Verification Levels

| Mutual Vouchers | Confidence |
|-----------------|------------|
| 2+ | High - auto-accept recommended |
| 1 | Medium - consider verifying |
| 0 | Low - verify out-of-band |

## Settings

### Recovery Threshold

Default: 3 vouchers required

Can be adjusted in Settings > Recovery Settings (not recommended to lower).

### Claim Expiration

Recovery claims expire after **7 days**. Generate a new claim if expired.

## Security Features

### In-Person Verification Required

Vouchers should only be created after verifying identity in person. This prevents:
- Impersonation attacks
- Social engineering

### Duplicate Voucher Prevention

Each contact can only vouch once per recovery claim.

### Conflict Detection

If two different new identities claim the same old identity:
- Contacts see a conflict warning
- Manual verification required
- Could indicate attack

### Revocation

If you recover your old device:
1. Sign a revocation with old private key
2. Recovery proof is invalidated
3. Contacts are notified

## What Is/Isn't Recovered

### Recovered

- Contact relationships
- Ability to communicate
- Contact cards (re-sent)

### NOT Recovered

- Old encryption keys (lost with device)
- Message history (can't decrypt)
- Notes and visibility rules you set
- Old shared secrets (replaced)

## Timeline Example

**Day 1**: Phone lost
**Day 2**: New phone, create identity, start recovery
**Day 3**: Meet Alice (vouch 1/3)
**Day 4**: Meet Bob (vouch 2/3)
**Day 5**: Meet Charlie (vouch 3/3) - Recovery complete
**Day 6+**: Contacts sync and accept your new identity

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid claim data"

- Claim may be corrupted
- Generate a new claim

### "Claim expired"

- Claims expire after 7 days
- Generate a new claim

### Contact can't vouch

- They must have your OLD public key as a contact
- Verify they knew your previous identity

### Low confidence warning

- Get vouchers from contacts the recipient knows
- Or verify out-of-band (call, meet in person)
