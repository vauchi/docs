# Contact Exchange Workflow

Step-by-step guide for exchanging contact cards with other Vauchi users.

## Overview

Vauchi uses in-person exchange to establish contact relationships. This ensures you only connect with people you've actually met, preventing spam and impersonation.

## Methods

### 1. QR Code Exchange (Recommended)

The primary method for exchanging contacts.

#### Steps for the Sharer

1. Open Vauchi
2. Tap the **Exchange** tab
3. Tap **Show My QR Code**
4. A QR code will appear with your contact information
5. Let the other person scan your code
6. Wait for proximity verification (audio handshake)
7. See "Exchange Successful" confirmation

#### Steps for the Scanner

1. Open Vauchi
2. Tap the **Exchange** tab
3. Tap **Scan QR Code**
4. Point camera at the other person's QR code
5. Wait for proximity verification
6. See "Exchange Successful" confirmation

### 2. Two-Way Exchange

Both people exchange cards simultaneously:

1. Person A shows their QR code
2. Person B scans it
3. Exchange completes (B receives A's card)
4. Person B shows their QR code
5. Person A scans it
6. Exchange completes (A receives B's card)

### 3. One-Way Sharing

To share your card without receiving theirs:

1. Open **Exchange** tab
2. Enable **Share Only** mode
3. Show your QR code
4. They scan and receive your card
5. You don't receive their card

## Proximity Verification

Vauchi verifies you're physically together using ultrasonic audio:

- Both phones emit and listen for audio handshake
- Range: approximately 2 meters
- If verification fails, exchange is blocked
- This prevents screenshot attacks

### Troubleshooting Proximity

If proximity verification fails:

1. Ensure both phones have working speakers/microphones
2. Move closer together (within 1-2 meters)
3. Ensure no loud background noise
4. Disable any audio-blocking apps
5. Try again

## QR Code Expiration

- QR codes expire after **5 minutes**
- Expired codes cannot be scanned
- Generate a new code if expired
- This prevents replay attacks

## After Exchange

Once exchange completes:

- The new contact appears in your **Contacts** list
- You can see their contact card
- They can see your contact card
- Future updates sync automatically

## Privacy Notes

- Only information on your card is shared
- You control what fields are visible (see [visibility workflow](2026-01-22-visibility.md))
- No personal data goes to servers - exchange is direct
- Relay only stores encrypted update messages

## Common Scenarios

### Meeting at a Conference

1. Both open Exchange tab
2. Take turns scanning QR codes
3. Both have each other's contact info
4. If they update their card later, you'll see the changes

### Sharing Business Card

1. Enable "Share Only" mode
2. Show QR code to recipients
3. They receive your professional contact info
4. You don't clutter your contacts

### Group Exchange

Exchange with multiple people:

1. Generate your QR code once
2. Let each person scan it
3. Generate new code after 5 minutes if needed
4. Scan their codes to complete two-way exchange
