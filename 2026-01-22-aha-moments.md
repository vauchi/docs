### **1. The In-Person Networking Moment**
*"I'm at a conference and meet someone interesting. Instead of fumbling for a business card or typing in a phone number, we just open Vauchi, scan each other's QR codes, and I feel my phone vibrate—confirming we're actually next to each other through ultrasonic verification. No fake QR codes from across the room. Real trust, established in seconds."*

### **13. The Forward Secrecy Moment**
*"A security-minded friend asked how Vauchi protects our messages. I explained: every update uses a new encryption key derived from the last one. Even if someone somehow got today's key, they couldn't decrypt yesterday's updates. It's called forward secrecy, and it happens automatically."*
**→ Aligns with:** Double ratchet protocol, chain keys, forward secrecy.
